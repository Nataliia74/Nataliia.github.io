About this event

Step into an enchanting afternoon at the Botanic Garden, where tradition meets elegance. As an esteemed guest, you will
be greeted with a complimentary welcome drink upon arrival from 4:00 pm to 7:00 pm, setting the perfect tone for an un
forgettable gathering. Immerse yourself in the serene beauty of the garden, savor a traditional Scottish afternoon tea,
and enjoy vibrant conversations in a charming ambiance. Connect with delightful individuals and expand your social network
in this semi-private, relaxed setting with cozy tables and sofas.

  • Dress code: casual
  • No entry deadline, but early arrival (before 4:00 pm) is recommended for optimal socializing.
  • Hosted until 7:00 pm, with guests invited to stay until closing.
  • Join us for a delightful escape to the tranquil and luxurious world of the Botanic Garden.
